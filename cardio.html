The Benefit of Cardio

Performance Enhancement

  • Improves work capacity between sets
  • Enhances recovery between workouts
  • Increases blood flow to muscles

Fat Loss Benefits

  • Creates additional calorie deficit
  • Boosts EPOC (afterburn effect)
  • Preserves muscle when dieting

Types for Lifters

  • LISS: Walking, cycling
  • HIIT: Sprints, circuits
  • Conditioning: Strongman cardio

Cardio Programming Tips

  • Schedule separate from lifting when possible
  • Post-workout is most time-efficient
  • Morning fasted cardio may enhance fat loss

Various cardio exercises to complement weight training

Heart Health Long Term

Cardiovascular Benefits

Blood Pressure

  • Reduces resting blood pressure
  • Improves arterial elasticity
  • Lowers risk of hypertension

Cholesterol Profile

  • Increases HDL (good cholesterol)
  • Decreases LDL oxidation
  • Improves triglyceride levels

Heart Function

  • Strengthens heart muscle
  • Lowers resting heart rate
  • Improves stroke volume

Even 30 minutes daily reduces cardiovascular disease risk by 40%

Cardio exercise strengthens the entire cardiovascular system, and benefit you for life.

How long to do Cardio?

General Health

  • 150 mins/week moderate
  • OR 75 mins/week vigorous
  • Spread across 3-5 sessions

Fat Loss Focus

  • 200-300 mins/week
  • Mix of HIIT and LISS
  • Increase gradually

For Strength Athletes

  • 2-3 sessions/week
  • 20-30 minutes/session
  • Low-impact preferred

Sample Weekly Plans

Beginner: 3x20min walks + 1x15min HIIT

Intermediate: 2x30min cycling + 2x20min HIIT

Advanced: 3x45min varied cardio + 1x30min conditioning

Tailor cardio duration to your specific fitness goal.
