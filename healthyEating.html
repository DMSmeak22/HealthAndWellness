Basic Nutrition

Fuel your weightlifting journey with these fundamental nutrition principles:

Protein Intake

1.6-2.2g per kg of body weight daily for muscle repair and growth. Sources include chicken, fish, eggs, and plant-based alternatives.

Carb Timing

Focus carbs around workouts for energy. Whole grains, fruits, and vegetables provide sustained energy.

Healthy Fats

Essential for hormone production. Include avocados, nuts, seeds, and olive oil in your diet.

The Macros

Macronutrient Breakdown

Protein (Muscle Builder)

  • Chicken breast: 31g per 100g
  • Salmon: 25g per 100g
  • Greek yogurt: 10g per 100g

Carbohydrates (Energy)

  • Sweet potato: 27g per 100g
  • Brown rice: 23g per 100g
  • Oats: 66g per 100g

Fats (Hormone Support)

  • Avocado: 15g per 100g
  • Almonds: 49g per 100g
  • Olive oil: 100g per 100g

Adjust ratios based on your goals: 40/30/30 for maintenance, 30/40/30 for bulking, 40/20/40 for cutting

These are examples of where you can get each macro. Most foods have more than 1, this chart examines the main macro in each food.

Basic Meal Ideas

Breakfast

  • 3 eggs + oatmeal + berries
  • Greek yogurt + granola + almond butter
  • Protein smoothie with banana and spinach

Lunch

  • Grilled chicken + quinoa + mixed veggies
  • Salmon + sweet potato + asparagus
  • Lean beef + brown rice + broccoli

Dinner

  • Turkey chili with beans
  • Shrimp stir-fry with vegetables
  • Baked cod with roasted potatoes

Snack Options

  • Cottage cheese with fruit
  • Protein bar (low sugar)
  • Handful of nuts with celery sticks
  • Hard-boiled eggs

Meal timing: Eat every 3-4 hours, with protein in each meal
